## What Is the Story of A Baby in a Basket?

Hey there! Welcome to an incredible adventure. I want to tell you a story about one of the bravest babies who ever lived. Yeah, you heard me right -- a brave BABY. His name? Moses. And his story starts in a really dark place.

This story comes from Exodus 1-2 and is part of the Old Testament.

## What Does This Story Teach Kids?

The story of A Baby in a Basket teaches children important lessons about freedom, courage, God's power, obedience. Here are the key takeaways for your child:

- **Trust in God** — Even when things seem impossible, God has a plan
- **Courage** — Standing up for what is right, even when it is hard
- **Faith** — Believing in God's promises even when you cannot see the outcome
- **Obedience** — Following God's instructions leads to blessings

## Quiz: Test What You Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### Why did Pharaoh order all Hebrew baby boys to be thrown into the Nile?

Pharaoh saw that the Israelites kept growing in number and he feared they might rise up against him, so he cruelly ordered all newborn Hebrew boys to be killed.

### What did Moses' mother do to save him?

After hiding Moses for three months, his mother made a basket, coated it with tar and pitch to make it waterproof, and placed him among the reeds of the Nile River.

### What amazing thing happened after Pharaoh's daughter found baby Moses?

Miriam cleverly offered to find a Hebrew woman to nurse the baby, and she brought Moses' own mother -- who then got paid by Pharaoh's daughter to raise her own son!

5. What does this story teach us about trusting God?
