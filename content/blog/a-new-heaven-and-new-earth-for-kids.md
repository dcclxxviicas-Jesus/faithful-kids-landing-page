## What Does This Story Teach Kids?

The story of A New Heaven and New Earth teaches children important lessons about faith, hope, heaven, God's promises, eternity. Here are the key takeaways for your child:

- **Trust in God** — Even when things seem impossible, God has a plan
- **Courage** — Standing up for what is right, even when it is hard
- **Faith** — Believing in God's promises even when you cannot see the outcome
- **Obedience** — Following God's instructions leads to blessings

## Quiz: Test What You Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### Who received the vision of the new heaven and new earth?

John — the beloved disciple of Jesus — was exiled on the island of Patmos when Jesus gave him the incredible vision recorded in the book of Revelation, including the new heaven and new earth.

### What did the voice from the throne promise about tears, death, and pain?

The promise is total and complete — God will wipe every tear from their eyes, and death, mourning, crying, and pain will be completely gone. Not reduced. Gone. The old order of things will have passed away forever.

### What did Jesus say from the throne about what He is doing?

Jesus said "I am making everything new" — not just fixing or repairing the old world, but creating something entirely new and perfect. This is the ultimate promise: everything broken will be made brand new.


## Discussion Questions for Parents

Use these questions at the dinner table or during bedtime to talk about the story with your child:

1. What was your favorite part of the story of A New Heaven and New Earth?
2. What do you think God was teaching us through this story?
3. How can we be more like A in our own lives?
4. If you were in this story, what would you have done?
5. What does this story teach us about trusting God?
