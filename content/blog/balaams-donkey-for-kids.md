## What Is the Story of Balaams Donkey?

Okay, I need you to stop whatever else you're thinking about because today's story has a TALKING DONKEY. Yes, you heard that right. A donkey that opens its mouth and has an actual conversation. This really happened. Let's get into it.

This story comes from Numbers 22-24 and is part of the Old Testament.

## What Does This Story Teach Kids?

The story of Balaams Donkey teaches children important lessons about courage, trust, following God, new beginnings. Here are the key takeaways for your child:

- **Trust in God** — Even when things seem impossible, God has a plan
- **Courage** — Standing up for what is right, even when it is hard
- **Faith** — Believing in God's promises even when you cannot see the outcome
- **Obedience** — Following God's instructions leads to blessings

## Key Bible Verse

> *"Trust in the Lord with all your heart, and do not lean on your own understanding."* — Proverbs 3:5

This verse connects to the story of Balaams Donkey because it reminds us that God's plan is always bigger than what we can see.

## Discussion Questions for Parents

Use these questions at the dinner table or during bedtime to talk about the story with your child:

1. What was your favorite part of the story of Balaams Donkey?
2. What do you think God was teaching us through this story?
3. How can we be more like Balaams in our own lives?
4. If you were in this story, what would you have done?
5. What does this story teach us about trusting God?
