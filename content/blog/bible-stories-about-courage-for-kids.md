# 10 Bible Stories About Courage for Kids (Be Brave Like David)

Looking for Bible stories that teach your child about courage? These 10 stories are the best examples of courage in the Bible, retold simply for kids ages 5+.

## Why Teach Kids About Courage Through Bible Stories?

Children learn values through stories, not lectures. When your child watches David face Goliath or Ruth stay loyal to Naomi, they are not just hearing about courage. They are seeing it in action. These stories give kids a vocabulary for courage that they carry into their own lives.

---

### 1. David and Goliath
A boy faces a giant.

### 2. Daniel in the Lions' Den
A man prays when it is illegal.

### 3. Esther
A queen risks her life for her people.

### 4. Moses at the Red Sea
Leading people toward the impossible.

### 5. Joshua at Jericho
Marching when it makes no sense.

### 6. Shadrach, Meshach, Abednego
Refusing to bow, even facing fire.

### 7. Gideon
A scared farmer becomes a warrior.

### 8. Peter Walks on Water
Stepping out of the boat.

### 9. Stephen
Standing firm even to the end.

### 10. Paul in Chains
Singing in prison.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best Bible story about courage for kids?

David and Goliath is the most popular Bible story about courage for children because it has a clear, memorable example that kids can understand and apply to their own lives.

### How do I teach my child about courage using the Bible?

Start with one story from this list. Watch the video together (60 seconds on Faithful Kids), then ask your child what they noticed about courage in the story. Connect it to something in their own life. Repeat with a new story each week.

### What age are these stories appropriate for?

These stories are designed for children ages 5-12. Younger children (5-7) will understand the basic lesson. Older children (8-12) can discuss the deeper implications and apply the lessons more independently.
