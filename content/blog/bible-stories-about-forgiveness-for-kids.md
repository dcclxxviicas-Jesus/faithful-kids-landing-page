# 8 Bible Stories About Forgiveness for Kids

Looking for Bible stories that teach your child about forgiveness? These 8 stories are the best examples of forgiveness in the Bible, retold simply for kids ages 5+.

## Why Teach Kids About Forgiveness Through Bible Stories?

Children learn values through stories, not lectures. When your child watches David face Goliath or Ruth stay loyal to Naomi, they are not just hearing about forgiveness. They are seeing it in action. These stories give kids a vocabulary for forgiveness that they carry into their own lives.

---

### 1. The Prodigal Son
A father forgives his wasteful son.

### 2. Joseph Forgives His Brothers
The brothers who sold him into slavery.

### 3. Jesus on the Cross
Father, forgive them, for they do not know what they are doing.

### 4. Peter's Restoration
Jesus forgives Peter for denying him three times.

### 5. David's Repentance
A king who sinned badly and came back to God.

### 6. Jonah and Nineveh
God forgives an entire city Jonah wanted destroyed.

### 7. The Unforgiving Servant
A man who was forgiven much refuses to forgive a little.

### 8. Stephen Forgives His Killers
Lord, do not hold this sin against them.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best Bible story about forgiveness for kids?

The Prodigal Son is the most popular Bible story about forgiveness for children because it has a clear, memorable example that kids can understand and apply to their own lives.

### How do I teach my child about forgiveness using the Bible?

Start with one story from this list. Watch the video together (60 seconds on Faithful Kids), then ask your child what they noticed about forgiveness in the story. Connect it to something in their own life. Repeat with a new story each week.

### What age are these stories appropriate for?

These stories are designed for children ages 5-12. Younger children (5-7) will understand the basic lesson. Older children (8-12) can discuss the deeper implications and apply the lessons more independently.
