# 8 Bible Stories About Friendship for Kids

Looking for Bible stories that teach your child about friendship? These 8 stories are the best examples of friendship in the Bible, retold simply for kids ages 5+.

## Why Teach Kids About Friendship Through Bible Stories?

Children learn values through stories, not lectures. When your child watches David face Goliath or Ruth stay loyal to Naomi, they are not just hearing about friendship. They are seeing it in action. These stories give kids a vocabulary for friendship that they carry into their own lives.

---

### 1. David and Jonathan
A prince protects his best friend from his own father.

### 2. Ruth and Naomi
Where you go, I will go.

### 3. Jesus and His Disciples
He called them friends, not servants.

### 4. Paul and Barnabas
Ministry partners who encouraged each other.

### 5. Moses and Aaron
Brothers who faced Pharaoh together.

### 6. Elijah and Elisha
A mentor passes the torch to a younger friend.

### 7. Mary and Elizabeth
Two women supporting each other through extraordinary pregnancies.

### 8. Paul and Timothy
A father-figure friendship across generations.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best Bible story about friendship for kids?

David and Jonathan is the most popular Bible story about friendship for children because it has a clear, memorable example that kids can understand and apply to their own lives.

### How do I teach my child about friendship using the Bible?

Start with one story from this list. Watch the video together (60 seconds on Faithful Kids), then ask your child what they noticed about friendship in the story. Connect it to something in their own life. Repeat with a new story each week.

### What age are these stories appropriate for?

These stories are designed for children ages 5-12. Younger children (5-7) will understand the basic lesson. Older children (8-12) can discuss the deeper implications and apply the lessons more independently.
