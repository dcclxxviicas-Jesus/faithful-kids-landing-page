# How to Teach the Bible in Homeschool: Complete Curriculum Guide

Teaching the Bible in your homeschool does not require a seminary degree. It requires a plan, good content, and consistency. This guide gives you a complete framework for teaching the entire Bible to your kids using 200 video lessons with quizzes.

Faithful Kids covers the entire Bible in 20 series of 10 episodes each. Here is how to structure it as a school year curriculum:

## Daily Lesson Plan (15-20 minutes)

1. **Watch** the day's video on Faithful Kids (60 seconds)
2. **Quiz** — take the comprehension quiz (2-3 minutes)
3. **Discuss** — use the discussion questions from the blog post (5 minutes)
4. **Activity** — coloring page, journal entry, or scripture copywork (5-10 minutes)
