# 20 Best Bible Stories for Sunday School (With Discussion Questions)

Running Sunday school and need ready-to-teach Bible stories? Here are the 20 best stories for children's ministry, each with a summary, key verse, 3 discussion questions, and a link to a free 60-second video on Faithful Kids.

## How to Use This List

Pick one story per week. Play the 60-second video. Read the key verse together. Ask the discussion questions. Done. Your entire Sunday school lesson in 10 minutes.

### 1. Creation (Genesis 1-2)
**Key verse:** "In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth." — Genesis 1:1
**Discussion:** What is your favorite thing God created? Why do you think God rested on Day 7? If you could ask God to create one more thing, what would it be?
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/in-the-beginning-creation-for-kids)

### 2. Noah's Ark (Genesis 6-9)
**Key verse:** "Noah did everything just as God commanded him." — Genesis 6:22
**Discussion:** Would you have built the ark if everyone laughed at you? What does the rainbow mean? When is it hard to obey God?
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/noah-and-the-great-flood-for-kids)

### 3. David and Goliath (1 Samuel 17)
**Key verse:** "I come against you in the name of the Lord Almighty." — 1 Samuel 17:45
**Discussion:** What is your "Goliath" right now? Why was David brave when everyone else was scared? How can God help you face big problems?
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/david-and-goliath-for-kids)

### 4. Daniel in the Lions' Den (Daniel 6)
**Key verse:** "My God sent his angel, and he shut the mouths of the lions." — Daniel 6:22
**Discussion:** Have you ever been in trouble for doing the right thing? Would you keep praying if it was against the rules? How did God protect Daniel?
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/daniel-in-the-lions-den-for-kids)

### 5. The Good Samaritan (Luke 10)
**Key verse:** "Go and do likewise." — Luke 10:37
**Discussion:** Who is someone you could help this week? Why did the two religious leaders walk past? What does it mean to be a good neighbor?
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/the-good-samaritan-for-kids)

### How long should a Sunday school lesson be?

For ages 5-7, aim for 15-20 minutes total. For ages 8-12, 20-30 minutes. A 60-second video + 5 minutes of discussion + a quiz fits perfectly within this window.
