# 15 Bible Stories for VBS (Vacation Bible School): Free Video Lessons

VBS coordinators: here are 15 ready-to-use Bible stories with free 60-second videos, quiz questions, and discussion prompts. Each story works as a standalone lesson or as part of a week-long VBS theme.

## 5-Day VBS Plan: "Heroes of Faith"

### Day 1: Noah — The Hero Who Obeyed
**Story:** Noah and the Great Flood | **Theme:** Obedience
**Video:** [Watch on Faithful Kids](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/noah-and-the-great-flood-for-kids)
**Key verse:** "Noah did everything just as God commanded him." — Genesis 6:22
**Discussion:** When is it hard to obey? What gave Noah the courage to keep building?

### Day 2: David — The Hero Who Was Brave
**Story:** David and Goliath | **Theme:** Courage
**Video:** [Watch on Faithful Kids](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/david-and-goliath-for-kids)
**Key verse:** "I come against you in the name of the Lord Almighty." — 1 Samuel 17:45
**Discussion:** What is your Goliath? How can God help you be brave?

### Day 3: Daniel — The Hero Who Prayed
**Story:** Daniel in the Lions' Den | **Theme:** Faith
**Video:** [Watch on Faithful Kids](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/daniel-in-the-lions-den-for-kids)
**Key verse:** "My God sent his angel, and he shut the mouths of the lions." — Daniel 6:22
**Discussion:** Have you ever been in trouble for doing the right thing?

### Day 4: Esther — The Hero Who Spoke Up
**Story:** Esther Saves Her People | **Theme:** Courage + Purpose
**Video:** [Watch on Faithful Kids](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/esther-saves-her-people-for-kids)
**Key verse:** "Who knows whether you have come to the kingdom for such a time as this?" — Esther 4:14
**Discussion:** Has there ever been a time when you were the right person to help?

### Day 5: Jesus — The Ultimate Hero
**Story:** The Resurrection | **Theme:** Hope
**Video:** [Watch on Faithful Kids](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/the-empty-tomb-for-kids)
**Key verse:** "He is not here; he has risen!" — Luke 24:6
**Discussion:** Why is the Resurrection the most important story in the Bible?
