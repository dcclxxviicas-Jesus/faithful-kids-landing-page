# The Christmas Story for Kids: 10 Bible Stories About the Birth of Jesus

The real Christmas story is better than any movie. Angels, a star, shepherds, wise men, and a baby born in the humblest place imaginable. Here are 10 Bible stories that tell the complete birth of Jesus, from the angel visiting Mary to the wise men following the star.

## The Complete Christmas Story in 10 Episodes

### 1. An Angel Visits Mary
The angel Gabriel appears to a young woman named Mary. "Do not be afraid. You will have a son, and you will call him Jesus." Mary says yes.
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/an-angel-visits-mary-for-kids)

### 2. Joseph and the Angel
Joseph is confused and afraid. An angel visits him in a dream: "Do not be afraid to take Mary as your wife. The child is from God."
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/joseph-and-the-angel-for-kids)

### 3. The Journey to Bethlehem
Mary and Joseph travel to Bethlehem for a census. Mary is about to have a baby. There is no room at the inn.
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/the-journey-to-bethlehem-for-kids)

### 4. Born in a Manger
Jesus is born in a stable. Mary wraps him in cloths and places him in a manger, a feeding trough for animals. The King of the universe enters the world in the humblest way possible.
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/born-in-a-manger-for-kids)

### 5. The Shepherds
Angels appear to shepherds in the fields at night. "Do not be afraid! I bring you good news of great joy!" The shepherds rush to see the baby.
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/the-shepherds-for-kids)

### 6. The Wise Men
Wise men from the East follow a star to find the newborn king. They bring gifts: gold, frankincense, and myrrh.
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/the-wise-men-for-kids)

### 7. Escape to Egypt
An angel warns Joseph: "Herod is searching for the child. Flee to Egypt." The family escapes in the night.
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/escape-to-egypt-for-kids)

### 8. Young Jesus in the Temple
Years later, 12-year-old Jesus is found in the temple, discussing Scripture with teachers. They are amazed by His understanding.
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/young-jesus-in-the-temple-for-kids)

### 9. John the Baptist
John prepares the way for Jesus, baptizing people in the river and telling everyone: "Someone greater than me is coming."
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/john-the-baptist-for-kids)

### 10. The Baptism of Jesus
*Note: The baptism happens about 30 years after Jesus' birth, but it completes the Birth of Jesus series by showing Jesus stepping into His public ministry.* Jesus comes to John to be baptized. As He comes out of the water, God's voice says: "This is my Son, whom I love."
[Watch video](https://faithfulkids.app/blog/the-baptism-of-jesus-for-kids)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### When should I teach my child the Christmas story?

Start in late November as Advent begins. Watch one episode per day during the 10 days before Christmas for a meaningful countdown.

### What is the real Christmas story in the Bible?

The birth of Jesus is told in Matthew 1-2 and Luke 1-2. It includes the angel visiting Mary, the journey to Bethlehem, Jesus born in a manger, shepherds, wise men, and the escape to Egypt.
