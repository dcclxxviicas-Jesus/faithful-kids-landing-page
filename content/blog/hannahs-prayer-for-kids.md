## What Is the Story of Hannahs Prayer?

Hey friend! Today's story is about a woman who was completely heartbroken — and a prayer so raw, so desperate, so real that it changed the course of an entire nation. This is the story of Hannah.

This story comes from 1 Samuel 1-2 and is part of the Old Testament.

## What Does This Story Teach Kids?

The story of Hannahs Prayer teaches children important lessons about bravery, loyalty, faith, God's plan. Here are the key takeaways for your child:

- **Trust in God** — Even when things seem impossible, God has a plan
- **Courage** — Standing up for what is right, even when it is hard
- **Faith** — Believing in God's promises even when you cannot see the outcome
- **Obedience** — Following God's instructions leads to blessings

## Key Bible Verse

> *"Trust in the Lord with all your heart, and do not lean on your own understanding."* — Proverbs 3:5

This verse connects to the story of Hannahs Prayer because it reminds us that God's plan is always bigger than what we can see.

## Discussion Questions for Parents

Use these questions at the dinner table or during bedtime to talk about the story with your child:

1. What was your favorite part of the story of Hannahs Prayer?
2. What do you think God was teaching us through this story?
3. How can we be more like Hannahs in our own lives?
4. If you were in this story, what would you have done?
5. What does this story teach us about trusting God?
