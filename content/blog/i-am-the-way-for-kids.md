## What Does This Story Teach Kids?

The story of I Am the Way teaches children important lessons about faith, hope, heaven, God's promises, eternity. Here are the key takeaways for your child:

- **Trust in God** — Even when things seem impossible, God has a plan
- **Courage** — Standing up for what is right, even when it is hard
- **Faith** — Believing in God's promises even when you cannot see the outcome
- **Obedience** — Following God's instructions leads to blessings

## Quiz: Test What You Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### What did Jesus tell His disciples not to let happen?

Jesus saw how afraid and worried His disciples were, so He told them, "Do not let your hearts be troubled. You believe in God — believe also in me." He wanted them to trust Him even when things looked scary.

### Which disciple asked, "How can we know the way?"

Thomas was the one who spoke up honestly and said, "Lord, we don't even know where you're going. How can we know the way?" And Jesus gave him one of the most famous answers in the Bible.

### When Jesus said "I am the way, the truth, and the life," what was He teaching?

Jesus didn't point to a system or a set of instructions. He said HE is the way to God. The path to the Father isn't a thing — it's a person. Knowing Jesus IS knowing God.


## Discussion Questions for Parents

Use these questions at the dinner table or during bedtime to talk about the story with your child:

1. What was your favorite part of the story of I Am the Way?
2. What do you think God was teaching us through this story?
3. How can we be more like I in our own lives?
4. If you were in this story, what would you have done?
5. What does this story teach us about trusting God?
