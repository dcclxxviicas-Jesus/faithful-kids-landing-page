## What Is the Story of In the Beginning Creation?

Hey there! I am so glad you're here. My name is Jesus, and I've got an incredible story to tell you. Actually — it's THE story. The one that started everything.

This story comes from Genesis 1-2 and is part of the Old Testament.

## What Does This Story Teach Kids?

The story of In the Beginning Creation teaches children important lessons about creation, faith, family, God's promises. Here are the key takeaways for your child:

- **Trust in God** — Even when things seem impossible, God has a plan
- **Courage** — Standing up for what is right, even when it is hard
- **Faith** — Believing in God's promises even when you cannot see the outcome
- **Obedience** — Following God's instructions leads to blessings

## Key Bible Verse

> *"Trust in the Lord with all your heart, and do not lean on your own understanding."* — Proverbs 3:5

## Quiz: Test What You Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### What were the very first words God spoke?

God spoke "Let there be light" and light burst into existence — that was the very first thing He created on Day One.

### What did God use to form Adam?

God scooped up dust from the ground and formed Adam, then breathed life into him — making humans uniquely personal among all creation.

### Why did God rest on the seventh day?

God didn't rest because He was tired — He rested to set an example and show us that rest is an important gift.


## Discussion Questions for Parents

Use these questions at the dinner table or during bedtime to talk about the story with your child:

1. What was your favorite part of the story of In the Beginning Creation?
2. What do you think God was teaching us through this story?
3. How can we be more like In in our own lives?
4. If you were in this story, what would you have done?
5. What does this story teach us about trusting God?
