## Quiz: Test What Your Child Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### Who was Jairus?

Jairus was an important synagogue leader who humbled himself, fell at Jesus' feet, and begged Him to come heal his dying daughter.

### What happened to the woman who touched Jesus' cloak?

The woman had been suffering from bleeding for twelve years and had spent all her money on doctors. The moment she touched Jesus' cloak in faith, she was instantly healed.

### What bad news did the messengers bring from Jairus's house?

Messengers came and told Jairus, "Your daughter is dead. Don't bother the teacher anymore."

### What did Jesus say to Jairus after the bad news?

Jesus looked right at Jairus and said, "Don't be afraid. Just believe." He refused to let the bad report be the final word.

### What did Jesus say to the little girl?

Jesus took her hand and said "Talitha koum," which means "Little girl, I say to you, get up." She immediately stood up and walked around.


## Discussion Questions for Families

4. If you were in this story, what would you have done differently?
5. What does this story teach us about trusting God?
