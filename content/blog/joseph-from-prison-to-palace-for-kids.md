## Quiz: Test What You Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### What did Pharaoh's two dreams mean?

God revealed through Joseph that Egypt would experience seven years of incredible harvests, followed by seven years of devastating famine. Joseph's plan to store grain during the good years saved the entire nation.

### What position did Pharaoh give Joseph after hearing the interpretation?

Pharaoh was so impressed with Joseph's wisdom and God-given insight that he made Joseph the second most powerful person in all of Egypt — going from prisoner to prime minister in a single day.

### What did Joseph tell his brothers about what they had done to him?

Instead of revenge, Joseph saw God's hand in everything — even the painful parts. He understood that God used the brothers' evil actions to put Joseph in a position to save many lives, including theirs.


## Discussion Questions for Parents

Use these questions at the dinner table or during bedtime to talk about the story with your child:

2. What do you think God was teaching us through this story?
3. How can we be more like Joseph in our own lives?
4. If you were in this story, what would you have done?
5. What does this story teach us about trusting God?
