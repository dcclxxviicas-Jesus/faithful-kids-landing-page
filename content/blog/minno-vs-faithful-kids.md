# Minno vs Faithful Kids: Which Bible App Is Better for Your Family?

Minno and Faithful Kids are the two most popular Bible-based video apps for kids. But they serve different purposes. Minno is entertainment. Faithful Kids is education. Here is an honest side-by-side comparison to help you decide.

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | Faithful Kids | Minno |
|---------|--------------|-------|
| **Price** | $9.99/mo | $9.99/mo |
| **Content type** | Short video lessons (60 sec) | Full animated shows (20-30 min) |
| **Bible coverage** | Genesis to Revelation (complete) | Select stories and themes |
| **Quizzes** | Yes, after every lesson | No |
| **Number of lessons** | 400+ | 100s of episodes |
| **Screen time controls** | Yes | No |
| **Parent dashboard** | Yes | No |
| **Best for** | Daily Bible learning | Entertainment / family movie night |

## When to Choose Faithful Kids

Choose Faithful Kids if you want your child to **learn the Bible systematically** with accountability. The quiz after every lesson means your child does not just passively watch. They engage with the content and prove they understood it. And with 400+ lessons covering the entire Bible, your child walks through Scripture from beginning to end.

## When to Choose Minno

Choose Minno if you want **Christian entertainment** that replaces Netflix or Disney+. Minno has full-length animated shows (VeggieTales, What's in the Bible) that are great for family movie nights or longer screen time sessions.

## Can You Use Both?

Yes, and many families do. Use Faithful Kids for **daily Bible learning** (one 60-second lesson + quiz per day) and Minno for **entertainment time** when you want a longer show. They complement each other well.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Minno or Faithful Kids better?

It depends on your goal. For Bible education with quizzes and full Bible coverage, Faithful Kids is better. For animated entertainment, Minno is better. Many families use both.

### Does Minno have quizzes?

No. Minno is a streaming service for shows. It does not have comprehension quizzes or progress tracking. Faithful Kids is the only Bible app with a quiz after every lesson.
