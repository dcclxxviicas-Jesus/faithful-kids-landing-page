The story of Peter is Restored teaches children important lessons about resurrection, hope, faith, new life, Easter. Here are the key takeaways for your child:

- **Courage** — Standing up for what is right, even when it is hard
- **Faith** — Believing in God's promises even when you cannot see the outcome
