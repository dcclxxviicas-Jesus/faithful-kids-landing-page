## What Does This Story Teach Kids?

The story of Solomons Proverbs teaches children important lessons about wisdom, worship, consequences, choices. Here are the key takeaways for your child:

- **Trust in God** — Even when things seem impossible, God has a plan
- **Courage** — Standing up for what is right, even when it is hard
- **Faith** — Believing in God's promises even when you cannot see the outcome
- **Obedience** — Following God's instructions leads to blessings

## Key Bible Verse

> *"Trust in the Lord with all your heart, and do not lean on your own understanding."* — Proverbs 3:5

This verse connects to the story of Solomons Proverbs because it reminds us that God's plan is always bigger than what we can see.

## Quiz: Test What You Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### According to Proverbs 3:5-6, what should you do instead of leaning on your own understanding?

Proverbs 3:5-6 tells us to trust in the Lord with all our heart and acknowledge Him in all our ways — and He will make our paths straight. God sees what we can't, so trusting Him is always the wisest move.

### What does "the fear of the Lord" mean in Proverbs 9:10?

"The fear of the Lord" doesn't mean being scared like God is a monster. It means having deep respect and standing in awe of who God is. That kind of reverence is where true wisdom begins.

### According to Proverbs 27:17, how does "iron sharpen iron"?

Just like a blade gets sharper when ground against another blade, good friends challenge each other to grow. Real friendship means pushing one another to be better, not just agreeing all the time.


## Discussion Questions for Parents

Use these questions at the dinner table or during bedtime to talk about the story with your child:

1. What was your favorite part of the story of Solomons Proverbs?
2. What do you think God was teaching us through this story?
3. How can we be more like Solomons in our own lives?
4. If you were in this story, what would you have done?
5. What does this story teach us about trusting God?
