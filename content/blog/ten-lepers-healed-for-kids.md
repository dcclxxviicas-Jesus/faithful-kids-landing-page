## Quiz: Test What You Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### How many men with leprosy called out to Jesus?

Ten men with leprosy stood at a distance and cried out, "Jesus, Master, have pity on us!"

### What did Jesus tell the ten men to do?

Jesus told them, "Go, show yourselves to the priests." You only did that after being healed — so it was a step of faith.

### When were the men healed?

As they were going — mid-step — their leprosy disappeared. They were healed on the way.

### How many of the healed men came back to thank Jesus?

Only one out of ten came back, threw himself at Jesus' feet, and praised God with a loud voice.

### What was special about the one man who returned?

The one who returned was a Samaritan — an outsider that most religious people looked down on. Yet he was the only one who came back to give thanks.


## Discussion Questions for Parents

Use these questions at the dinner table or during bedtime to talk about the story with your child:

1. What was your favorite part of the story of Ten Lepers Healed?
2. What do you think God was teaching us through this story?
3. How can we be more like Ten in our own lives?
4. If you were in this story, what would you have done?
5. What does this story teach us about trusting God?
