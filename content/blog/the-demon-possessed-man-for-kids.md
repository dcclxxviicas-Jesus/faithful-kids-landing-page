## Quiz: Test What You Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### Where did the demon-possessed man live?

The man lived among the tombs — the burial places for the dead. He was completely isolated from normal life.

### What name did the demons give when Jesus asked?

The demons answered, "My name is Legion — for we are many." A Roman legion was thousands of soldiers, telling us there were thousands of demons.

### What did the demons beg Jesus to let them enter?

The demons begged Jesus to send them into a large herd of about two thousand pigs feeding on a nearby hillside.

### How did the townspeople react when they saw the man healed?

The townspeople were terrified when they saw the man sitting calmly and in his right mind. They actually asked Jesus to leave their region.

### What did Jesus tell the healed man to do?

Jesus told him, "Go home to your own people. Tell them how much the Lord has done for you." The man's testimony became his mission.
