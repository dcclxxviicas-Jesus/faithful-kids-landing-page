## What Is the Story of The Fiery Furnace for Kids?

EXILE & FAITH — EPISODE 4: THE FIERY FURNACE Based on Daniel 3 | Narrated by Jesus

This is one of the most jaw-dropping stories in the entire Bible. It has a ninety-foot golden statue, a furious king, a furnace cranked to maximum heat, and a mystery figure walking through the flames. Let's dive in.

After his dream about the statue, you'd think Nebuchadnezzar would have learned some humility. Nope. Instead, he builds a NINETY-FOOT golden statue of himself and commands every single person in the empire to bow down and worship it the moment the music plays. Anyone who refuses gets thrown into a blazing furnace.

The music plays. Everyone in the kingdom drops to their knees.

This story comes from Daniel/Jonah/Isaiah and is part of the Old Testament.

## Parent's Guide: Why This Story Matters

Every Bible story is an opportunity to talk with your child about faith in a natural, low-pressure way. After watching, ask an open-ended question like 'What was your favorite part?' or 'What do you think God was trying to teach us?' These conversations do not need to be long or perfect. The goal is simply to make the Bible feel like a living, relevant part of your family's life, not something dusty on a shelf.
