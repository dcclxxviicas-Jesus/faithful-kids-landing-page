## What Is the Story of The Garden and the Fall?

Welcome back, friend! Last time we watched God create the entire universe. Today? Things take a turn. This is the story of the most beautiful place that ever existed — and the day everything changed.

This story comes from Genesis 2-3 and is part of the Old Testament.

## What Does This Story Teach Kids?

The story of The Garden and the Fall teaches children important lessons about creation, faith, family, God's promises. Here are the key takeaways for your child:

- **Trust in God** — Even when things seem impossible, God has a plan
- **Courage** — Standing up for what is right, even when it is hard
- **Faith** — Believing in God's promises even when you cannot see the outcome
- **Obedience** — Following God's instructions leads to blessings

## Key Bible Verse

## Quiz: Test What You Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### What was the ONE rule God gave Adam and Eve?

Out of all the thousands of trees in the garden, God only told them to stay away from one — the Tree of the Knowledge of Good and Evil.

### How did the serpent trick Eve?

The serpent twisted what God actually said, making Eve doubt God's word. He told her she wouldn't die and that she'd become like God.

### When God asked "Where are you?", what was He really doing?

God knows everything — He wasn't lost. The question was an invitation, giving Adam and Eve a chance to be honest and come back to Him.
