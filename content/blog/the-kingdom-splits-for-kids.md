## Quiz: Test What Your Child Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### What did the people of Israel ask Rehoboam to do when he became king?

The people were exhausted from Solomon's heavy taxes and forced labor. They asked Rehoboam for a lighter load, promising they would serve him faithfully in return. It was a reasonable request.

### Whose advice did Rehoboam follow?

Rehoboam rejected the wise counsel of his father's experienced advisors — who told him to be kind — and instead listened to his young friends, who told him to be even harsher than Solomon. It was a catastrophic choice.

### What happened after Rehoboam's harsh response to the people?

Ten tribes rebelled and formed the Northern Kingdom (Israel) under Jeroboam. Only Judah and Benjamin stayed with Rehoboam in the Southern Kingdom (Judah). The united kingdom was permanently torn apart.
