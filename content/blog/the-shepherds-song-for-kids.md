## What Is the Story of The Shepherds Song for Kids?

KING DAVID'S REIGN — EPISODE 5: THE SHEPHERD'S SONG — PSALM 23 Based on Psalm 23 | Narrated by Jesus

Friends, we have been through an incredible journey with King David. Now I want to end with the most famous thing David ever wrote. Six verses that have comforted millions for three thousand years. Psalm 23.

To understand this psalm, remember who wrote it. David was not a poet in a library. He was a shepherd boy from Bethlehem who protected his father's sheep from lions and bears with his bare hands. When David wrote about a shepherd, he knew exactly what he meant.

"The Lord is my shepherd, I shall not want."

This story comes from 2 Samuel/Psalms and is part of the Old Testament.

## Parent's Guide: Why This Story Matters

The birth of Jesus is the story your child will hear every Christmas for the rest of their life. Giving them the real version now, not just the cultural one, matters. Jesus was not born in a palace. He was born in the humblest place imaginable, to an ordinary young woman, announced first to shepherds (the lowest status people in society). Every detail of this story says: God is for everyone, especially the overlooked.
