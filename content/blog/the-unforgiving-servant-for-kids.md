## Quiz: Test What You Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### When Peter asked how many times he should forgive, what did Jesus answer?

Peter thought seven times was generous, but Jesus said seventy times seven — not as a literal number to count to, but to show that forgiveness should have no limit. Stop counting and just keep forgiving.

### After the king forgave the servant's massive debt, what did that servant do?

Even though the servant had just been forgiven millions, he turned around and choked a fellow servant over a tiny amount. He received incredible mercy but refused to show any to someone else.

### What is the main lesson of this parable?

The whole point is that God has forgiven each of us a debt we could never repay. Compared to that, anything someone else has done to us is small. If we have been forgiven an ocean, we can forgive a puddle.
