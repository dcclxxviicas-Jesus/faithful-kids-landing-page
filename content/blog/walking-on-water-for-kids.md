## Quiz: Test What Your Child Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### Why did Jesus send the disciples ahead in the boat without Him?

After feeding the five thousand, Jesus went up on a mountainside by Himself to pray while the disciples crossed the lake.

### What time of night did Jesus walk on the water toward the boat?

The Bible says it was during the "fourth watch of the night," which was between 3 and 6 AM — the darkest, most exhausting hours.

### What did the disciples think they were seeing when Jesus walked on the water?

The disciples were terrified and screamed, "It's a ghost!" They couldn't believe what they were seeing.

### What happened when Peter took his eyes off Jesus?

When Peter looked at the wind and waves instead of keeping his eyes on Jesus, fear took over and he began to sink.

### What did the disciples say after Jesus and Peter got into the boat?

The disciples worshipped Jesus and declared, "Truly you are the Son of God." The miracle revealed His true identity.
