## What Is the Story of Young Jesus in the Temple?

Okay, this next story is one of my favorites — because it's about ME as a kid. That's right. I was twelve years old, and I already knew exactly who I was.

This story comes from Luke 2:41-52 and is part of the New Testament.

## What Does This Story Teach Kids?

The story of Young Jesus in the Temple teaches children important lessons about hope, faith, God's plan, Christmas, miracles. Here are the key takeaways for your child:

- **Trust in God** — Even when things seem impossible, God has a plan
- **Courage** — Standing up for what is right, even when it is hard
- **Faith** — Believing in God's promises even when you cannot see the outcome
- **Obedience** — Following God's instructions leads to blessings

## Key Bible Verse

> *"Trust in the Lord with all your heart, and do not lean on your own understanding."* — Proverbs 3:5

This verse connects to the story of Young Jesus in the Temple because it reminds us that God's plan is always bigger than what we can see.

## Quiz: Test What You Learned

After watching the video, see if your child can answer these questions:

### How old was Jesus when His family went to Jerusalem for Passover in this story?

Jesus was twelve years old when this event happened — still a kid, but already showing incredible wisdom and a deep connection with God.

### What was Jesus doing when Mary and Joseph finally found Him in the temple?

Jesus was sitting among the greatest religious teachers in Israel, asking deep questions and giving answers that amazed everyone who heard Him.

### What did Jesus mean when He said "Didn't you know I had to be in my Father's house?"

Even at twelve, Jesus understood that God was His true Father and the temple was where He belonged. He already knew His purpose and identity.


## Discussion Questions for Parents

Use these questions at the dinner table or during bedtime to talk about the story with your child:

1. What was your favorite part of the story of Young Jesus in the Temple?
2. What do you think God was teaching us through this story?
3. How can we be more like Young in our own lives?
4. If you were in this story, what would you have done?
5. What does this story teach us about trusting God?
